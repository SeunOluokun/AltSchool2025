Biography

My name is Oluwaseun Oluokun. Seun for short.

I studied medical physiology at the University of Lagos College of Medicine.

After graduating university in 2019 , I worked for a year as a medical cardiologist and worked hard... at trying not to hate the job so much..

Don't get me wrong , it was fun playing with medical devices and giving patients gist while perfoming their cardiology scans..

I however didn't like what i was doing day to day .. I also did not find that i had any time for myself as i worked Monday - Saturday.


I started programming in 2021 after seeing a SheCodes course on YouTube. I was soon after learnt SQL and got a job as a Database Developer late 2022.

It's been 2 years in this same role, and I have grown tired, feeling stuck and itching for a change. I know I can't get this change without Upskilling formally.

So here I am, Ready to Upskill and Welcome New Challenges!

Why I Joined Alt School

I joined the Alt School of Engineering program because I want to enhance my technical skills, network with like-minded individuals, and gain hands-on experience to solve real-world challenges. The program's structured learning path aligns perfectly with my aspirations to become a proficient software engineer.

My Goals

Through the School of Engineering program, my goals are:

  • Build projects that showcase my technical expertise.
  • Transition into a fulfilling career in software engineering.
  • Use my skills to contribute to social change initiatives.
